Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chemistry Flashcards. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

2. Information We Collect

This website is designed to be privacy-first and collects minimal data:

  • Local Storage Only: Your flashcard progress, study history, and preferences are stored only in your browser's local storage
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, emails, or personal information
  • No Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Your study progress is stored locally on your device to maintain your learning history
  • Data is never transmitted to external servers
  • All data remains under your complete control

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it remains secure on your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser and operating system updated
  • Using a secure internet connection when accessing the site
  • Clearing your browser data if using a shared device

5. Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and may use:

  • GitHub: For hosting (subject to GitHub's privacy policy)
  • Analytics: We do not currently track user behavior

6. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for educational use by A-Level students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents who believe their child has provided information on this site should contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the GitHub repository or email.
